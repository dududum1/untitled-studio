## Washi

- **Washi W**: High contrast with a fibrous texture, blind to red light.
  - *Background*: Hand-coated onto Japanese Kozo paper.
  - *Use Case*: Experimental, highly textured fine art B&W.
- **Washi D**: Punchy, industrial panchromatic look with thin base light leaks.
  - *Background*: Converted Russian aerial surveillance film.
  - *Use Case*: Gritty industrial and urban landscapes.
- **Washi S**: Surgical, ultra-fine grain with extreme contrast.
  - *Background*: Optical sound recording film for motion pictures.
  - *Use Case*: High-contrast architectural and abstract photography.
- **Washi X**: Warm, technical hues with noticeable bloom.
  - *Background*: Traffic surveillance film cross-processed in C41.
  - *Use Case*: Retro, off-color street photography.
- **Washi Z**: Pitch black skies and glowing white foliage.
  - *Background*: Near-infrared mapping film.
  - *Use Case*: Surreal, dramatic landscape photography.
- **Film Washi Z**: Alternate processing of Washi Z with extreme contrast and crushed shadows.
  - *Background*: Deep infrared aerial stock.
  - *Use Case*: High-impact, graphic nature shots.
- **Film Washi S**: Alternate processing of Washi S with crushed blacks and zero grain.
  - *Background*: Motion picture sound film.
  - *Use Case*: Stark silhouettes and abstract lines.
- **Film Washi X**: Zero grain, glowing highlights, no anti-halation layer.
  - *Background*: Surveillance stock.
  - *Use Case*: Dreamy, high-contrast night street photography.

- **Hanalogital Solaris**: Procedural galaxy/nebula overlays with shifted shadows.
  - *Background*: Experimental "film soup" soaked in chemicals.

- **Hanalogital Renegade**: Red lightning bolts and electrical discharge.

  - *Background*: Pre-exposed light-streak film.

- **Hanalogital Bokeh**: Generates colorful, out-of-focus light blobs.
  - *Background*: Pre-exposed with bokeh shapes.
  - *Use Case*: Dreamy, romantic portrait overlays.
- **Hanalogital Veins**: Red and purple lightning-like veins across the image.
  - *Background*: Static-discharge experimental film.
  - *Use Case*: Horror or surrealist editorial.

  - *Use Case*: Fine art landscape and architecture.
- **Delta 400**: Punchy contrast, sharp grain, modern look.
  - *Background*: Faster sibling to Delta 100.
  - *Use Case*: Action, street, and editorial B&W.
- **FP4 Plus 125**: Classic, medium contrast, fine grain.
  - *Background*: Traditional cubic-grain emulsion.
  - *Use Case*: High-quality studio and outdoor portraits.
- **Pan F Plus 50**: Extreme contrast, virtually invisible grain.
  - *Background*: Ultra-slow film for massive enlargements.
  - *Use Case*: Macro photography and stark, sunny landscapes.
- **SFX 200**: Deep contrast, near-infrared sensitivity.
  - *Background*: Traffic surveillance film adapted for creative use.

- **HP5 Single / XP2 Single**: Gritty, high contrast, heavy vignette.
  - *Background*: Emulates Ilford's single-use disposable B&W cameras.

  - *Background*: Ilford's brief, historic foray into consumer color film.
  - *Use Case*: Retro, unassuming snapshot aesthetics.
